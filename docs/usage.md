# Usage

## Recording a meeting

1. Open GOATLY and sign in if prompted.
2. Click the **microphone button** to start recording. The button turns red while recording is active.
3. When the meeting ends, click **Stop**. On mobile (iOS/Android) you will be asked to consent to AI data processing the first time.
4. GOATLY automatically uploads the audio to FAL Wizper for transcription.

## Viewing the transcript

Once transcription completes the meeting card shows the full transcript. Tap/click the card to expand it.

## Generating a summary

After transcription, click **Summarize** on the meeting card. GOATLY sends the transcript to an LLM (via OpenRouter) and returns a bulleted summary of key points.

## Search

Use the **search bar** at the top to filter meetings by any word in the title or transcript. Results update as you type.

## Sort

Use the **sort control** (date or name) to reorder your meeting list.

## Deleting a recording

Swipe a card (mobile) or use the delete icon (desktop) to remove a recording. This deletes the local WAV file and the database entry.
